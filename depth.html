  • Shanghai | CityWalk

    [City Walk] Suzhou River – The Mother River of Shanghai

    [City Walk] Suzhou River – The Mother River of Shanghai

    The starting point of the city and the source of life: Flowing downstream, the modern Bund and the traditional Shikumen are not so distinctly separate; their blend is a microcosm of the birth of Shanghai culture. Old warehouses full of stories: Beneath the red bricks lies the struggle of the Rong family to become China’s first national capitalists, and the warehouse stores secrets of Du Yuesheng becoming a prominent figure. The Sihang Warehouse still remembers the indomitable spirit of the Eight Hundred Heroes. Countless "Far East Firsts": "Far East's First Post Office", "Far East's First Apartment Building", "Far East's First Hotel"... Let's reminisce about the glories of a bygone golden era.

    Starting at 138 RMB

Your personal exotic cooking journey is a deep dive into Thailand.
